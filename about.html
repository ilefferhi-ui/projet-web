About Us

Welcome to the Lost & Found Campus Manouba platform — a dedicated online service designed to help students, staff, and faculty of the University of Manouba reconnect with their lost belongings quickly and efficiently.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to make the process of reporting and recovering lost items on campus as easy and stress-free as possible. We believe that losing something valuable should not be a permanent setback, and that a connected campus community can work together to return lost items to their rightful owners.

Who We Are

We are a team of computer science students from ENSI (Ecole Nationale des Sciences de l'Informatique), University of Manouba. This platform was developed as part of our web development course project, with the goal of solving a real problem faced by our campus community every day.

How It Works

Our platform allows users to submit reports of lost or found items, browse through listed items using our search feature, and get in touch with the person who found or lost an item. The process is straightforward, free, and available to all members of the university.

Why We Built This

Every day, dozens of items are lost across campus — phones, keys, student cards, laptops, and more. Without a centralized system, recovering these items was nearly impossible. We built this platform to bridge that gap and give every student and staff member a reliable tool to report and recover their belongings.
